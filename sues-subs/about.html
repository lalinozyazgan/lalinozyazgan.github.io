Our Story

Sue's Sandwiches has been serving the best sandwiches to the San Diego community since 2010.


It all started when Sue Myers was laid off from her corporate job and decided to pursue her dream of owning a sandwich shop for the people.


Sue wanted to make a different kind of sandwich - the highest quality sandwiches for the community she grew up in.


We source the freshest local ingredients and make them in house when possible, including bread baked fresh everyday. Our menu changes depending on what's in season.
